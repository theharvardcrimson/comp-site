Tech associates collaborate with News and other boards to work on collecting data through web scraping and data analysis, and turn the data into beautiful graphics to be featured on our website. The comp will teach you how to use Pandas and BeautifulSoup.

We want the Crimson website to be user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing for our online readers. You will learn how to work with Figma to improve all users' experiences on thecrimson.com.

The Tech Comp is designed to accommodate programmers with all levels of experience—many have joined the board before even finishing CS50, and others have come on with years of web programming experience.
